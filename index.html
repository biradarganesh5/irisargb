How It Works

IRIS ARGB Controller uses an ESP32 running WLED firmware to drive addressable LEDs with precision and flexibility.

A lightweight web UI exposes per-LED control and effect presets, allowing you to customize every aspect of your lighting without installing bloated PC software.

SignalRGB and OpenRGB detect it as a WLED device over the network, enabling PC-wide synchronization with your RAM, motherboard, and other RGB components for a unified lighting experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any PC software?
No. The controller has 100+ effects built-in and can be controlled via mobile app or web browser.
Optional: use SignalRGB/OpenRGB if you want system-wide synchronization with other RGB devices.
Can it control fans and LED strips together?
Yes, as long as they are 5V addressable (3-pin) ARGB devices. The SATA power ensures stable current delivery within the limits of your PSU.
Does it work with Alexa?
Yes, you can expose effects and segments to Alexa via supported integrations and create custom routines for voice control.
What's the difference between the X3, X6, and X10 models?
Only the number of ARGB ports. All models have identical performance, per-LED control, 100+ effects, and all features. Choose based on how many RGB devices you need to connect.
